I am an Assistant Professor in the East Asian College at Yonsei University and an international security scholar specializing in empirical research on interstate conflict and diplomacy.

My research applies experimental and computational social science methods to examine conflict escalation and de-escalation, particularly in the context of U.S.–China great power competition.

My work has been published in *International Studies Quarterly*, *Journal of Conflict Resolution*, *The Journal of Experimental Political Science*, *Political Science Research and Methods*, and *Research & Politics*.

I received my Ph.D. in Political Science from Florida State University in 2024. During the 2023–2024 academic year, I was the Lo Chia-Luen International Sinology Visiting Fellow at National Chengchi University.
